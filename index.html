About us
Why work with us
Since 1988

We have been organizing behind-the-scenes tours and special experiences for performing arts organizations and others non profits.

 

 

 

Personalized care

We have a reputation based on the knowledge, personalized care and warmth with which our programs are planned and carried out.

 

 

 

 

Strengthen bonds

We have helped more than 50 regional and historic theatres and performing arts organizations throughout the country strengthen bonds with their financial supporters.

Architectural preservation

We have had a role in saving Grand Central Terminal, Radio City Music Hall, the New Amsterdam Theater, 10 historic districts, and 35 Broadway Theaters allowing us to share the importance of supporting architectural preservation.
