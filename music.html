Music

Exclusive entrée into concert halls, gospel churches, private jazz jam sessions.

  • A visit to Carnegie Hall, including a private meeting with Carnegie Hall’s dynamic, mesmerizing, and knowledgeable archivist/historian.
  • The opportunity to join a select audience for a Classical Music Concert on a Private Houseboat, including a private meeting with the owner of the houseboat and producer of New York’s longest-running chamber music series.
  • A Sunday Morning Gospel Service officiated by one of Harlem’s most influential pastors and featuring one of Harlem’s finest Gospel Choirs. Private meeting with a Harlem-born member of the church, who – after the service – will take us on a walking exploration of the neighborhood in which she was raised.
  • A private Jazz Jam Session in the Parlor of a Lovely Harlem Brownstone.
  • A private Broadway musical entertainment with cast members from different hit Broadway musicals.  
  • A backstage visit at the Metropolitan Opera House, including a private meeting with an Opera Expert and Metropolitan Opera Supernumerary.
  • A private visit to a Harlem school featured in the film “Music of the Heart,” and the Academy Award Nominated documentary “Small Wonders.”
