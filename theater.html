Theater

Broadway Adventures with behind the scenes access to theatres and principal members of the Broadway community.

  • A V.I.P. Tours of New York Exclusive:  You can choose to have the entire group see the same show, or you can give your guests the opportunity to choose their own shows. 
  • We will obtain the equivalent of house seats (or better) for each show chosen.
  • A private Broadway entertainment and in-depth discussion with cast members, each from a different hit Broadway musical.
  • Private discussions with Broadway producers, directors, costume designers, set designers, company managers, theater critics, etc. 
  • Private behind-the-scenes visits to historic Broadway theaters, concert hall, and motion picture palaces including: the New Amsterdam, Radio City Music Hall, the Apollo, Carnegie Hall, Lincoln Center, the Brooklyn Academy of Music, the Brooklyn Kings, among many others.
  • A private visit to Rosie’s Theater Kids, which provides training in dance, music and drama to students attending New York City public schools.
  • A private visit to a Broadway Costume Shop, as hosted by a costumiére.
  • Meet Select Members of the Cast following a Broadway Performance.
  • A private visit to the world’s first performing arts complex (1908), still one of New York’s premiere cultural venues for Dance, Music, and Theatre. Including private meetings with Executive and Artistic Staff members.
